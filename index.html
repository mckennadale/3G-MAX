We are a family kennel based in Dublin that has one objective in life and that is to produce quality XL American Bullies, Our life revolves around our dogs and this is evident in the pups we produce. We have over 10 years’ experience with American Bullies and in this time we have dedicated are lives to this breed and want you to share with us the beauty of this dog.




BloodLines

Max 3G Bullies have imported some of the finest Bully bloodlines in the world to Ireland with the aim of producing the finest XL American Bullies Ireland has ever seen. Our dogs have various top kennel bloodlines such as Bossy Kennels & Crakk Kennels both of which produce stunning examples of the breed from temperament to overall quality and structure of the dog.

Health

The health of our dogs is paramount here at Max 3G where are dogs are not just animals they are family. Our dogs undergo regular health checks to ensure overall health and well-being. This goes into every pup Max 3G produces, this is not a kennel that just wants to make money we want to produce the best examples of this breed and show to the world how wonderful this breed really is.
